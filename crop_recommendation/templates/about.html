The Project


Our project is an innovative smart farming solution designed to empower Indian farmers through the power of IoT and machine learning. By seamlessly integrating advanced sensors for soil nutrients (NPK), pH levels, temperature, and humidity, our system creates a live stream of vital agricultural data. This information is analyzed using intelligent ML algorithms to offer accurate and personalized crop recommendations based on local soil and climate conditions.
